full list of supported instructions

r8  - 8-bit register
r16 - 16-bit register
imm - immediate value
()  - dereference

mnemonic | first operand | second operand
-----------------------------------------
MOV        r8              imm
MOV        r16             imm
MOV        r8              r8
MOV        r16             r16
MOV        (imm)           r8
MOV        (imm)           r16
